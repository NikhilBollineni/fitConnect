Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

XA ("we", "our", or "us") operates the XA mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account and use XA:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and role (trainer or member) provided during sign-up.
  • Profile Data: Age, height, weight, fitness goals, bio, and location that you optionally provide.
  • Workout Data: Exercise logs, workout plans, nutrition plans, sets, reps, and weights you record.
  • Body Weight Logs: Weight entries you track over time.
  • Messages: Chat messages exchanged between trainers and clients within the app.
  • Subscription Data: Purchase history managed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store via RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (workout tracking, plan management, coaching).
  • To connect trainers with their clients via invite codes.
  • To display your progress analytics and workout history.
  • To send push notifications about workout reviews, messages, and plan updates.
  • To process subscriptions through the app stores.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore database and Firebase Authentication). Data is hosted on Google Cloud servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and cloud functions.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and in-app purchase processing.
  • Expo / EAS: App building and over-the-air updates.
  • OpenAI: AI-powered workout logging features.

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data handling.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data may be shared in these limited cases:

  • Trainer-Client relationship: When you connect with a trainer, they can see your profile, workout logs, and plan data.
  • Marketplace: If you opt in to be visible to trainers, your basic profile is shown in the discovery feature.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt out of the trainer marketplace visibility.

8. Children's Privacy

XA is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email.
